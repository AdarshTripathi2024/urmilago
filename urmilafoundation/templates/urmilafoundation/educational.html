Urmila Foundation Educational Activities

At Urmila Foundation, we believe education is the most powerful tool to break the cycle of poverty and build a brighter future. Our educational programs are tailored to support children, youth, and women — especially from marginalized communities.

School Kit Distribution

We provide free school bags, books, uniforms, and stationery to underprivileged children, ensuring they never miss school due to lack of resources.

Digital & Skill-Based Education

We organize computer literacy programs and skill-based workshops that prepare students for future careers and promote digital awareness in rural areas.

Awareness & Motivation Campaigns

We conduct regular awareness campaigns encouraging parents to send their children, especially girls, to school — highlighting the importance of consistent education.

Scholarship & Sponsorship Programs

Through our scholarship initiatives, we support meritorious but financially challenged students to continue their higher education in schools, colleges, and vocational institutions.

  • Career Counseling for Rural Youth
  • Awareness Campaigns on Polio etc
  • Menstrual Health & Hygiene Sessions
  • Voting Awareness Drives
  • Safe Touch / Unsafe Touch Sessions
  • Poster-Making & Essay Competitions
