About Urmila Foundation

Urmila Foundation is a charitable trust established with the vision of building a healthier, more equitable, and sustainable future for all. Backed by one of the region’s most respected hospitals, our foundation is deeply rooted in compassion and a strong sense of social responsibility.

At Urmila Foundation, we strive to be a beacon of hope and a bridge to a better life. Guided by empathy and driven by action, we continue to touch lives and transform communities—one step at a time.

Our mission is to uplift communities through initiatives focused on healthcare, child education, women empowerment, poverty alleviation, and environmental conservation.

We strieve to:


  • Offer medical services to those who cannot afford them.
  • Run food & Nutrition programs to fight hunger & malnutrition.
  • Promote women empowerment and skill development.

We envision a world where every child has access to education, every individual receives medical care, and every community thrives with dignity and opportunity. Our efforts are directed toward creating long-term change by building self-sustaining communities.


  • 100+ free medical treatments provided.
  • 50+ women empowered through skill development.
  • 200+ children educated through our programs.

Our Field of Services

At Urmila Foundation, our work spans across critical areas that lay the foundation for a stronger, healthier, and more compassionate society. Each of our service fields is guided by real need, deep empathy, and a commitment to sustainable impact.

poverty Alleviation

We work with marginalized families to create pathways out of poverty through livelihood training, essential aid, and long-term community support.

Medical Care

Contribute to our Campaign to ensure access to essential medical treatments, medicines, and healthcare services for those in need, saving lives every day.

Child Education

We support a child's future by contributing to his/her education programs, providing books, school supplies, and scholarships for underprivileged children.

Healthy Food

Help us provide nutritious meals to children and families in need and passing their life on roads as beggars, ensuring no one goes to bed hungry.

Nature Conservation

Our environmental programs focus on tree plantation, waste reduction, and sustainability awareness, fostering a cleaner and greener tomorrow.

Women Empowerment

From skill development programs to health awareness, we empower women to become confident contributors and leaders in their communities.
