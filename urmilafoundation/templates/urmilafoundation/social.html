Urmila Foundation Social Activities

Urmila Foundation has been tirelessly working to uplift the underprivileged through various social initiatives that create long-lasting impact. Our social activities are driven by compassion, empathy, and a deep commitment to human dignity.

Health Camps & Medical Aid

We regularly organize free health check-up camps, blood donation drives, and medical assistance programs in rural and underserved areas. From distributing essential medicines to supporting critical treatments — we ensure no one is left behind.

Child Education Drives

Our team conducts awareness campaigns promoting education, distributes school kits, and supports underprivileged children with scholarships and digital learning tools.

Women Empowerment Workshops

We host workshops focused on vocational training, self-help groups, and leadership development — enabling women to gain financial independence and social confidence.

Environmental Activities

We actively engage in tree plantation drives, cleanliness campaigns, and plastic-free awareness programs — fostering a sustainable and green future.

  • Animal Welfare & Feeding Drives
  • Rainwater Harvesting Awareness
  • Old-Age Home Visits
  • Free Health Screening Camps
  • Blood Donation Camps
  • Cleanliness Programs
